## Detailed description

BreezeFill makes web forms feel effortless.

It quietly suggests the details you use most often, like your name, email, phone number, links, and education info, right when a form asks for them. When you enter something new, BreezeFill can ask if you want to remember it for next time instead of saving it behind your back.

Designed for real web forms:

- Suggests profile details for common fields like name, email, phone, website, LinkedIn, GitHub, school, degree, and graduation info
- Learns site-specific answers and can reuse them the next time the same field appears
- Works across modern form patterns including iframes, open shadow DOM, and native select fields
- Handles major job application form ecosystems with extra matching logic for application-style pages
- Stores data locally in your browser so you stay in control

Great for:

- job applications
- contact and lead forms
- account setup pages
- profile editors
- internship and scholarship applications
