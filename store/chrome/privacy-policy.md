# BreezeFill Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

BreezeFill is designed to help you autofill web forms while keeping your data under your control.

## What BreezeFill stores

BreezeFill stores the profile details and saved form responses that you choose to keep, such as:

- name
- email address
- phone number
- website or social links
- address details
- education details
- site-specific saved responses
- muted-site preferences

## Where data is stored

BreezeFill stores this information locally in your browser using the browser extension storage APIs.

BreezeFill does not send your saved form data to a BreezeFill server because BreezeFill does not operate a backend service for syncing or processing your autofill data.

## How BreezeFill uses data

BreezeFill uses stored data only to:

- suggest autofill values on pages you visit
- remember responses after you explicitly save them
- keep track of settings like whether prompts are enabled

## Data sharing

BreezeFill does not sell your personal information.

BreezeFill does not share your saved autofill data with third parties.

## Permissions

BreezeFill requests access to websites because it needs to detect compatible form fields and show autofill suggestions on the pages where you want to use it.

BreezeFill requests storage permission so it can save your profile values, remembered responses, and settings.

## Your choices

You can:

- edit or delete your saved profile data at any time
- remove individual saved responses
- clear all remembered values
- mute specific sites
- disable the extension entirely
