Quick checklist

1. Load the extension unpacked and open the BreezeFill popup or settings page.
2. Add a name, email, or phone number to your profile.
3. Focus the matching field here and confirm the suggestion appears quietly.
4. Type a different value, leave the form or submit it, and confirm the save prompt appears.
